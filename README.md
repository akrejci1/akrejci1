# Adam Krejčí

Master’s student in Economic Policy and International Relations at Masaryk University, focused on quantitative analysis, international political economy, and applied research. I use R, Python, SQL, and Excel to work with economic, political, and financial data.

I have experience with research data collection, structured screening, and dataset cleaning from my work at the Academy of Sciences of the Czech Republic. Beyond quantitative work, I have experience with qualitative and comparative policy research. My bachelor's thesis examined governance and monitoring frameworks for Green Public Procurement across the US, EU, and China, combining cross-country institutional analysis with geopolitical perspectives on cleantech competition.
